WORKSHOPS & TALKS

Isis is a senior press officer at leading creative agency Inside/Out who works on innovative and exciting campaigns that traverse a wide breadth of genres from avant-garde to pop.

Recent notable acts include Anna Meredith OBE, who was shortlisted for the 2020 Mercury Hyundai Prize for her album FIBS, and - in stark contrast in style - rising pop star Ashnikko. Current and past clients and campaigns include; Somerset House Studios, Southbank Centre’s Concrete Late series, the city wide RE-TEXTURED Festival, NAKED CITY, Warp Records, RVNG Intl., and many many more.

To reach her at Inside/Out, contact: isis@insideout.agency
